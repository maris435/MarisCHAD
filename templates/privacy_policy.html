Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect the information provided by users of my web application ("MarisCHAD"). I am committed to ensuring the privacy and confidentiality of your information.

Information Collection and Use

The Webapp is designed to not collect any personally identifiable information ("Personal Data"). The app does not require users to provide their names, email addresses, or any other personal information.

Anonymous User Questions

When you use the Webapp, you may submit questions anonymously. These questions will be stored in a database for the sole purpose of providing the service you requested. We do not associate these questions with any personally identifiable information.

Data Security

Reasonable precautions were taken to protect the information stored in the database. An appropriate level of procedures were implemented to safeguard and secure the collected information. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and I cannot guarantee absolute security so please refrain from entering any personal data.

Data Retention

We retain the anonymous user questions in our database for a reasonable period necessary to provide the service and improve our Webapp. After this period, we will delete or anonymize the questions to ensure they cannot be linked back to any individual.

Prohibition of Sharing App Author's Personal Data

We strictly prohibit any sharing, disclosure, or distribution of the personal data belonging to the author of this app. The app author's personal data includes but is not limited to their name, contact information, and any other personally identifiable information. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of the app author's personal data and take appropriate measures to protect it from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Third-Party Services

The Webapp, deployed on Heroku and using OpenAI's ChatGPT API does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal data.

Cookies

The app does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies

Children's Privacy

The Webapp is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.If such data is discovered, it will be promptly deleted.
